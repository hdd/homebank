Balances

One of the essential part of your accounting attention will be the balances. HomeBank offers you 3 different one, covering then the full time-line: past, present and future.

Bank

is the past balance
which is the sum of all the validated transactions, (checked with the last and prior bank statement).

Today

is the present balance
which include transactions with a date lower or equal today.

Future

is the future balance
which include every transactions.

These balances are detailed for each account in the main window and are also summed globally.
It is also reminded when you open an account in the account window.

At last, remind that there is no countinuous balance column in the account window, which display the list of transactions for a specific account. The overdrawn report should be used if you need to check balance phased in ordered date.
