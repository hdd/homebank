Using the car cost

The carcost is an optional feature that focus on your cars spend and consumption of fuel.

In order to be able to see something in the carcost report window, the following step must be done:

1. Create some car categories

Let's say you have 2 cars:
- you create a category called 'Car' - you then create 2 subcategories that will be your cars: Peugeot 106 Sport, Peugeot 206, or whatever

Optionnaly you can set the default car to be inited into the carcost report window from the wallet dialog.

2. Fill in the transactions with necessary datas

First, set the corresponding category field for every of your spent for a car (refuel, repair,...)

Fill in additionnal datas in the description at every of your refuel:

  • [d=] meter: is the distance the meter indicate at the refuel time
  • [v=] fuel: is the volume of fuel you filling into the car tank
Example:
- '(d=92458 v=15.41)' - 'd=92458, v=15.41' - 'd=92458 v=15.41'

3. watch the results in the carcost report

Please see the car cost report for the details.
