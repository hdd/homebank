Car cost report

The car cost report will focus on your vehicle costs including fuel and repairs. It also display miscellaneous information like fuel consumption ans others.

Please refer to the using the car cost section for details on how using this feature.

Window call

This report can be called in the following way:

  • from the main window-menu: Reports / Car cost
  • from the main window-tool-bar.

Using car cost report

Display

Car category

select the category used for your car costs

Date filter

From / To

specify date bound limit to restrict the results to

Range / Month / Year

fast select a date with predefined range, or specific month and/or year

Global results

Meter

the meter of the vehicle

Consumption

fuel consumption

Fuel cost

cost of the consumed fuel

Other cost

the other cost for the car (insurance, repair, ...), which mean the transaction affected to the current carcost group not containing (d=xx v=xx)

Total cost

the total cost for the car in the selected period

Detailed results

his is a list-view which contains the detailed date by date refuel done for the selected car.
The list is sortable, just click into the column title to change the sort order.
At the bottom is the total line, except for price which is the average price by liter.
